Ila's story begins in a tiny room in Moti Vavdi where you will see lots of lovely designs on cushions, bedcovers, pillows, and more. From the beginning, life presented its challenges. Ila Sachani overcame her physical challenges to excel in her craft. Ila was born with a deformity that rendered her arms useless. Ila Sachani's story showed determination, and patience. Her mother and grandmother were experts in Kathiawar emroidery and decided to share this art with Ila. At first, this was just a hidden talent, she was doing this for herself. Soon people started to notice her talent. They admired her work. Eventually, this word reached the government office in Surat. They were impressed by her dedication and work. They displayed some samples of her emroidery at a state exhibition. People noticed the beauty of her work. Ila was no longer a hidden talent. Ila encouraged by this popularity, started participation in exhibitions at the national level! She received many awards not just for her artbut for her creativity and became receiver of the president's medal. She proved that challenges can turned into victory through focus. She also influenced people like her into doing what they love and enjoy.
